Packing Tips for Your Safari

What should you bring for your East African adventure? Here's a quick list of essentials:

  • Lightweight, breathable clothing in neutral colors
  • Comfortable walking shoes or safari boots
  • Sunscreen, hat, and sunglasses
  • Binoculars and camera with extra batteries
  • Travel adapter (UK-style plugs)
  • Personal medication and a basic first aid kit

Pro tip: Pack light and use soft duffel bags for small bush planes.
